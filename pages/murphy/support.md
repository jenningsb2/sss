## Common questions

**My workouts aren't syncing between my iPhone and Apple&nbsp;Watch.**
Make sure both devices are signed into the same iCloud account and that iCloud Drive is enabled. Murphy uses your private iCloud database — workouts can take up to a minute to sync after a session&nbsp;finishes.

**The watch app says it can't track my&nbsp;run.**
Murphy needs Location ("When In Use") and Motion & Fitness access on the watch to measure mile splits via GPS. Check Watch → Settings → Privacy &&nbsp;Security.

**I tipped but nothing&nbsp;happened.**
Tips are gratitude, not unlocks — there is no premium content in Murphy. Thank&nbsp;you. 🇺🇸
